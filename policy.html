Return and Refund Policy


Following the terms and conditions outlined in this Return and Refund Policy, Vegana ensures the buyer's rights by allowing them to request a product return or refund as follows:

  1. Buyers at Vegana can request a refund or return the purchased items by clicking the "Request Return/Refund" button on the Vegana website within 7 (seven) days from the receipt marked "Delivered" on Vegana's system.
  2. Upon requesting a return or refund, Vegana's buyers must return the purchased product to Vegana's Returns Processing Center within 6 (six) calendar days from the approved return/refund request date. When returning a Vegana-purchased product, the buyer must pack it with or include the original packaging, affixing the provided return slip ("Return Slip") by Vegana.
  3. Vegana carefully reviews each return/refund request and reserves the right to make the final decision based on the condition of the product returned to Vegana.

At its discretion, Vegana may reject acceptance of the returned Vegana product (and associated return and refund requests) if it:

  • Does not meet the Return Conditions;
  • The Return/Refund Request is completed after the Return Request Period has ended;
  • The Vegana product is returned in a manner inconsistent with point b) above;
  • The returned Vegana product is damaged due to the buyer's error, including cases where damage occurred due to improper packaging by the buyer for return.

Shipping and handling fees for return shipments: Currently, Vegana sponsors all shipping and handling costs for return shipments to Vegana.
